Privacy Policy — Ch'ti Discuss

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Data Controller

IROKOO — Sylvain Boutet
Contact: sylvain@irokoo.fr

2. Data Collected

Ch'ti Discuss does not collect any personal data directly. The application connects to your own Odoo server and all data (messages, channels, attachments) is stored exclusively on that server.

  • Login credentials: stored locally on your device to maintain the session.
  • Messages and attachments: sent only to your configured Odoo server.
  • Device language: detected to display the interface in the correct language.

3. Notifications

The application may send push notifications to alert you of new messages. No notification data is shared with third parties.

4. Data Storage

Session credentials are stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage. No data is stored on third-party servers.

5. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. All communications are exclusively between the application and your Odoo server.

6. Your Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify and delete your data. Contact your Odoo administrator or write to sylvain@irokoo.fr.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated. The last modification date is indicated at the top of this page.
